### Material Composition
- **Grade**: 304, 316
- **Chemical Composition**: 
  - **Grade 304**: 
    - Chromium (Cr): 18-20%
    - Nickel (Ni): 8-10.5%
    - Manganese (Mn): ≤2%
    - Carbon (C): ≤0.08%
    - Silicon (Si): ≤1%
  - **Grade 316**:
    - Chromium (Cr): 16-18%
    - Nickel (Ni): 10-14%
    - Molybdenum (Mo): 2-3%
    - Manganese (Mn): ≤2%
    - Carbon (C): ≤0.08%
    - Silicon (Si): ≤1%
